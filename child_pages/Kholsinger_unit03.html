My life with my little cat family

There's always been a place for cats in my heart

I have always loved cats since I was little mostly because they were cute and quiet and I would always get jealous of my friends who had them. My interest in cats grew as I got older which is why had a lot of t-shirts with cats on them. Over the five years of being with my fiancé I have been helping take care of our 4 cats. My fiancé has had cats in her life ever since she was born but got a cat that was just for her at three years old, this cat's name is Barbie. After about a year and a half of dating we adopted two cats, a brother and sister named Neki and Cho. About a year later we adopted an albino kitty named Yuki.

Our four Cats

Cho
A 4 year old American Shorthair Tuxedo; sister to Neki
Yuki

Barbie

Taking care of the cats

There comes a lot of responsibility with owning 4 cats, some chores include

  • Cleaning litter boxes
  • Grooming the cats
  • Feeding and medicating the cats

Each cat gets fed seperatly due to food allergies and medication.

  • Dry cat food brands
    • Royal Canin
    • Iam's
  • Wet Cat food brands
    • Tiki Cat
    • Fancy Feast

Feeding Barbie her meals

  1. Get Barbie a clean food bowl
  2. Put her clean food bowl on her mini table
  3. Give her half a can of Fancy Feast Savory Centers pate into her bowl
  4. Pat down her food to make it easier for her to eat

Feeding Yuki, Neki, and Cho breakfast

  1. Feed Yuki
    • Give Yuki a 1/4th a cup of her Royal Canin chicken only dry food into her microchip feeder
  2. Feed Neki
    • Give Neki a 1/4th a cup of iams dry food into his microchip feeder
  3. Feed Cho
    • Give Cho a 1/4th a cup of iams dry food into her microchip feeder
    • Give her one of her calming treats

Feeding Yuki, Neki, and Cho dinner

  1. Feed Yuki
    • Give Yuki her Tiki Cat chicken only wet food into her microchip feeder
    • Give her a pill pocket with a benadryl inside
  2. Feed Neki
    • Give Neki half a can of Fancy Feast Savory Centers pate into his microchip feeder
  3. Feed Cho
    • Give Cho a whole can of Fancy Feast Savory Centers pate into her microchip feeder
    • Give her one of her calming treats
